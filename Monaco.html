Upcoming F1 Grand Prix in Monaco

The iconic Monaco Grand Prix will be the seventh event on the 2023 Formula 1 calendar. With the drivers having to navigate the famously narrow streets of Monte Carlo, the race is famed for being one of the most demanding on the schedule. In 2022, Sergio Perez claimed his first win of the season after navigating mixed conditions, with Carlos Sainz and Max Verstappen joining him on the podium.
