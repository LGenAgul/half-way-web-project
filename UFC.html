Upcoming fight between Mcgregor and Chandler

McGregor is marking ten years as a UFC fighter in 2023 but it’s been 18 months since fans have seen the Irishman in the Octagon and over three years have passed since his last win. The extended absence from the sport is now McGregor’s second longest period without an MMA bout following the near-two-year gap he took for a crossover boxing match against Floyd Mayweather. The former lightweight champion will now be welcomed back at welterweight, with three-time Bellator 155lbs king Chandler bulking up for the bout to match McGregor’s new physique. Both men will work as coaches in The Ultimate Fighter season 31 before squaring off against each other in late 2023. A victory for McGregor would put him on a path to dethrone Brit star Leon Edwards if he hopes to become the first three-weight champion in UFC history.
